To ensure the security and confidentiality of our financial transactions, access to our past performance data is restricted to users who have established a verified payment instrument.

Please provide Proof of Funds (POF) using one of the following secure banking methods:

  • Standby Letter of Credit (SBLC)
  • Documentary Letter of Credit (DLC)
  • MT103 Transfer

Required Proof of Funds Methods:

  • MT799: Secure message indicating the readiness and availability of funds.
  • MT199: Standard message confirming bank acknowledgment of funds.
  • Bank Comfort Letter (BCL) + Account Transaction Verification (ATV): Letter from your bank confirming funds, with transaction verification.
  • RAW + ATV: Detailed account statement with transaction verification.

Please coordinate with your financial institution to provide the necessary documentation. Once verified, you will gain access to our past performance data.
